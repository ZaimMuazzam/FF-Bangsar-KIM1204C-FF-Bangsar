Chocolate Frappuccino

Frappe or called “frap-pay,” is the name for a blended iced chocolate that produces a delicious, frothy and refreshing drink. Whipping cream is also added on top and served with or without seasoning such as caramel drizzle, choclate chips or nuts. It is served cold after grinding it with ice cubes
